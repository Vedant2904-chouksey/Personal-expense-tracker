# <Personal expense tracker>

# Problem Statement

Many individual find it difficult to track and manage their daily expenses and income efficiently or easily.
Traditional methods such as hard copy records or spreadsheets are tiresome, easily error prone, and lack real-time insights.
This makes budgeting, spending control, and financial planning difficult, resulting in poor financial decisions and lack of awareness about spending patterns.

# Scope of the Project

The personal expense tracker project targets to provide a digital solution for managing ,  organizing, and analyzing personal financial transactions.
The tracker support the features of adding, viewing income and expense entries.
It will generate summaries , notes and visualizations to help users understand their spending habits, set budgets, and receive timely prompts and alerts to aid in effective financial management.
